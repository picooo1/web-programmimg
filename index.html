Biography

Fei Fei Li is a prominent computer scientist
known for her groundbreaking work in
artificial intelligence and computer vision.
She is the co-founder of AI4ALL,
a nonprofit organization
aiming to increase diversity and inclusion
in the field of artificial intelligence.
Additionally, Li is a professor at Stanford University
and the director of the Stanford Artificial Intelligence Lab,
where her research focuses on machine learning
and its applications to healthcare and robotics.
